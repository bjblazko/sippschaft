# Harold Smith
The progenitor of the clan. A WWII veteran and farmer.

## Biography
Harold grew up in rural Kansas and developed a love for the land at an early age. He served in the US Navy during WWII, stationed in the Pacific. After the war, he returned home to take over the family farm.

### Education
- **High School**: Smallville High, Class of 1943.

### Hobbies
- Woodworking
- Fishing at the local creek
- Collecting vintage tractors

### Places Lived
- Smallville, KS (1925-1943, 1946-2005)
- San Diego, CA (Navy posting, 1943-1945)
