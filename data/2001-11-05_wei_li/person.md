# Li Wei
Partner of William Brown.

## Biography
Wei grew up in Shanghai, China. She is a classical violinist who tours internationally. She values her heritage and conducts workshops on traditional Chinese music. She met William when he attended one of her concerts while studying.

### Education
- **Conservatory**: Shanghai Conservatory of Music.
- **Master's**: Juilliard School, NYC.
