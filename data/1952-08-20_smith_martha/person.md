# Martha Smith
Matriarch of the Smith family. She was a school teacher for 30 years.

## Biography
Martha was a dedicated educator who taught history at the local high school. She inspired generations of students to look beyond their small town. She was an avid reader and founded the local book club.

### Education
- **University**: University of Kansas, BA in History, 1974.

### Hobbies
- Reading historical fiction
- Traveling (visited all 50 states)
- Playing piano

### Places Lived
- Wichita, KS (Childhood)
- Lawrence, KS (University)
- Smallville, KS (Since 1975)
