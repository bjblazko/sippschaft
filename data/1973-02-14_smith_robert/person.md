# Robert Smith
Eldest son of Arthur and Martha. He is an engineer. He was simplified married to Linda, and later to Susan.

## Biography
Robert showed an aptitude for mechanics early on, often taking apart his father's tools. He became a civil engineer and worked on major bridge projects across the state. His personal life was more turbulent, but he remains a devoted father to both his children.
