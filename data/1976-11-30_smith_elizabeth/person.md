# Elizabeth Smith
Daughter of Arthur and Martha. She is a doctor.

## Biography
Elizabeth was always the studious one. She became a pediatrician and opened her own clinic in Smallville, wanting to give back to the community that raised her. She married her high school sweetheart, Thomas.

### Education
- **Medical School**: University of Kansas School of Medicine, MD, 2002.

### Hobbies
- Gardening
- Pottery
- Volunteering at the animal shelter

### Places Lived
- Smallville, KS (Lifelong resident, except for medical school in Kansas City)
