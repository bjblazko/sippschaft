# Arthur Smith
Patriarch of the Smith family. Born in the mid-20th century, he worked as a carpenter.

## Biography
Arthur inherited his father's love for working with his hands but preferred timber to soil. He built his own house in 1975 where he raised his family. He was known for his quiet wisdom and dry humor.

### Education
- **Vocational School**: Kansas State Tech (Carpentry), 1970.

### Hobbies
- Carving wooden ducks
- Watching baseball
- Hiking

### Places Lived
- Smallville, KS (1950-1968)
- Topeka, KS (1968-1972)
- Smallville, KS (1972-2022)
