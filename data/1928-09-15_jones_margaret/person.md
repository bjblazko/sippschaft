# Margaret Jones
The matriarch. Known for her prize-winning pies.

## Biography
Margaret was born into a large family of bakers. She met Harold at a county fair in 1947. She was the heart of the community, organizing charity drives and Sunday get-togethers.

### Education
- **High School**: Mill Creek High, Class of 1946.

### Hobbies
- Baking (won Blue Ribbon 5 years in a row)
- Quilting
- Gardening

### Places Lived
- Mill Creek, KS (1928-1948)
- Smallville, KS (1948-2010)
