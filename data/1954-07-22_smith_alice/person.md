# Alice Smith
Arthur's younger sister. She moved to the city and met Frank.

## Biography
Alice was the adventurous one. She left the family farm to pursue a career in journalism in Chicago. She covered local politics for the Tribune before retiring to write mystery novels.

### Education
- **University**: Northwestern University, Journalism, 1976.

### Hobbies
- Writing crime thrillers
- Jazz music
- Urban gardening

### Places Lived
- Smallville, KS (Until 1972)
- Chicago, IL (1972-Present)
